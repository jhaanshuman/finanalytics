# FinAnalytics Monetization Model

## Pricing Tiers

### Free Tier
- Up to 100 transactions/month
- Basic dashboard
- 3 chart types
- CSV import only
- Community support

### Pro ($9.99/month)
- Unlimited transactions
- Full dashboard suite
- 50+ chart types
- All import formats
- AI Copilot (100 queries/month)
- Email support
- 2 workspaces

### Business ($29.99/month)
- Everything in Pro
- AI Copilot (unlimited)
- Advanced analytics
- Team collaboration (up to 5)
- API access
- Priority support
- 10 workspaces
- Custom integrations

### Enterprise ($99.99/month)
- Everything in Business
- Unlimited team members
- White-label option
- Dedicated AI training
- On-premise deployment
- SLA guarantee
- Custom development
- 24/7 phone support

## Revenue Streams

1. **Subscription Revenue** - Monthly/annual plans
2. **Plugin Marketplace** - Third-party integrations (30% commission)
3. **API Usage** - Per-request pricing for developers
4. **Professional Services** - Custom implementation
5. **Data Insights** - Anonymized market research (opt-in)
6. **White Label** - Enterprise branding

## License Model
- **Personal**: One device, personal use only
- **Professional**: Up to 3 devices, business use
- **Enterprise**: Unlimited devices, organization-wide
- **OEM**: Embedded in third-party products
